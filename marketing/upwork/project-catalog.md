## FAQs

**How long does it take?**
Starter goes live in about 7 days, Professional in about 14, and the full multi-provider Premium build in roughly 30. Timelines start once I have your content, styles, and Stripe details.

**Do I need to know how to code?**
No. You run your business; I handle every technical piece — build, Stripe, deployment, the domain. You get a walkthrough and plain-language docs at the end.

**Who owns the code?**
You do. Every package includes the full source code and a clean handover. There's no lock-in and no monthly license to me.

**How do the deposits work, and what are the fees?**
Clients pay a deposit through Stripe to lock their slot; they pay the remaining balance in person. The deposit goes to **your** Stripe account — I take no cut of your bookings. Stripe charges its standard processing fee (typically ~2.9% + 30¢ in the US); that's paid to Stripe, not to me.

**Can it use my own brand and domain?**
Yes. Your colors, your styling, and your domain. On the Premium tier I set up the custom domain for you; on lower tiers I deploy to a live URL you control and can point a domain at.

**Do you offer support or maintenance after launch?**
Yes. Premium includes a priority support window, and I offer ongoing support and maintenance as a monthly add-on for any tier. You're not left on your own after handover.

**I'm not a braider — can it work for my business?**
Absolutely. The platform fits any appointment-based beauty or service business: barbers, lash and nail techs, tattoo artists, makeup artists, personal trainers, tutors. Anywhere clients book time and you want a deposit up front, this fits.

**What does hosting cost me long-term?**
The app deploys on Vercel, which has a free tier that covers many small businesses; heavier traffic moves to their paid plan (starts around $20/month). Stripe is pay-as-you-go per transaction. I'll walk you through exactly what applies to your setup — no surprise bills.
